Your Lie In April
Kosei Arima

Kousei Arima (有馬 公生 Arima Kōsei) is the main protagonist of the Your Lie in April series. He is the son of Saki and Takahiko Arima, who attends Sumiya Junior High School, and was the youngest winner of the Maihou Piano Competition.

About
Kaori Miyazono

Kaori was a free-spirited, gifted violinist whose playing style reflected on her personality. She attended Sumiya Junior High School as a third-year-student and was in the same class as Tsubaki Sawabe. She first met Kousei Arima when she asked Tsubaki to set her up with Ryota Watari. As their friendship grew, she eventually began helping Kousei return to the piano world after his mother's death.

Tsubaki Sawabe

Tsubaki Sawabe (澤部 椿 Sawabe Tsubaki?) is one of the main characters of the Shigatsu wa Kimi no Uso series. She is a third-year at Sumiya Junior High School.
